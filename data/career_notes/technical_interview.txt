# Technical Interview Preparation

## Coding Interview

### Data Structures to Master
- Arrays and Strings
- Linked Lists
- Stacks and Queues
- Trees (Binary, BST, Trie)
- Graphs
- Hash Tables
- Heaps

### Algorithms to Know
- Sorting (Quick, Merge, Heap)
- Binary Search
- BFS / DFS
- Dynamic Programming
- Two Pointers
- Sliding Window
- Backtracking

### Problem Solving Framework
1. Clarify the problem (5 min)
2. Explore examples (5 min)
3. Break down the problem (5 min)
4. Code the solution (15-20 min)
5. Test with examples (5 min)
6. Optimize if needed (5 min)

## System Design Interview

### Key Concepts
- Load balancing
- Caching strategies
- Database sharding
- API design
- Microservices vs monolith
- Message queues
- CDN

### Framework
1. Requirements clarification
2. High-level design
3. Detailed design
4. Scaling and optimization

## Behavioral Interview

### STAR Method Examples

#### Leadership
"Tell me about a time you led a difficult project"
- Situation: Describe the context
- Task: Your responsibility
- Action: What you did
- Result: Positive outcome with metrics

#### Teamwork
"Describe a time you worked with a difficult team member"
- Focus on communication and compromise
- Show empathy and professionalism

#### Problem-Solving
"Tell me about a time you solved a complex problem"
- Show your analytical process
- Highlight creative solutions

## Common Questions

1. Why this company?
2. Why this role?
3. What are your strengths/weaknesses?
4. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
5. Tell me about a failure and what you learned
