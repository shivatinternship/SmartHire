# Interview Preparation Guide

## Common Interview Types

### 1. Behavioral Interview
- Use STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- Prepare stories from past experiences
- Focus on leadership, teamwork, problem-solving
- Practice out loud

### 2. Technical Interview
- Review core concepts
- Practice coding problems
- Think out loud
- Ask clarifying questions

### 3. Case Study Interview
- Structure your approach
- Ask good questions
- Show analytical thinking
- Present recommendations clearly

## STAR Method Template

### Situation
Describe the context and background.

### Task
Explain your responsibility or challenge.

### Action
Detail the specific steps you took.

### Result
Share the outcome and impact.

## Salary Negotiation Tips

### Preparation
1. Research market rates
2. Know your worth
3. Consider total compensation
4. Practice your pitch

### During Negotiation
1. Be confident but polite
2. Provide specific numbers
3. Consider non-salary benefits
4. Get offer in writing
