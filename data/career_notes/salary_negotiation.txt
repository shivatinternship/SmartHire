# Salary Negotiation Guide

## Before Negotiating

### Research Market Rates
- Use Glassdoor, Levels.fyi, LinkedIn Salary
- Check company-specific data
- Consider location adjustments
- Factor in benefits and equity

### Know Your Value
- Document your achievements
- Quantify your impact with numbers
- List unique skills and certifications
- Consider your experience level

## During Negotiation

### Key Strategies
1. Always provide a specific number, not a range
2. Negotiate the total compensation package
3. Use silence as a tool - let them respond first
4. Get competing offers when possible
5. Be enthusiastic about the role while negotiating

### What to Negotiate
- Base salary
- Signing bonus
- Annual bonus structure
- Stock options / RSUs
- Vacation days
- Remote work flexibility
- Professional development budget
- Title

### Script Template
"Thank you for the offer! I'm very excited about this opportunity. Based on my research and experience, I was expecting something closer to [amount]. Is there flexibility in the compensation package?"

## Common Mistakes

1. Accepting the first offer immediately
2. Not negotiating at all
3. Making ultimatums
4. Lying about other offers
5. Negotiating via email when possible to do it in person/call

## After Accepting

1. Get the final offer in writing
2. Express gratitude
3. Don't share your salary with colleagues
4. Focus on proving your value in the new role
