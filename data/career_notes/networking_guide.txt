# Networking and LinkedIn Guide

## Building Your Professional Network

### Why Networking Matters
- 70-80% of jobs are filled through networking
- Builds long-term career relationships
- Provides mentorship opportunities
- Increases industry visibility

### Networking Strategies

#### Online
- Optimize your LinkedIn profile
- Share industry insights regularly
- Comment on others' posts thoughtfully
- Join relevant LinkedIn groups
- Send personalized connection requests

#### Offline
- Attend industry meetups and conferences
- Join professional associations
- Participate in hackathons
- Volunteer for industry events
- Alumni network events

## LinkedIn Profile Optimization

### Headline
- Don't just list your job title
- Include key skills and value proposition
- Example: "Data Engineer | Python & SQL Expert | Building Scalable Data Pipelines"

### About Section
- Tell your professional story
- Include keywords for search
- End with a call to action

### Experience
- Use bullet points with action verbs
- Quantify achievements
- Include relevant keywords

### Skills & Endorsements
- List top 50 skills
- Pin your most important 3
- Request endorsements from colleagues

### Recommendations
- Request 3-5 recommendations
- Offer to write recommendations in return
- Diverse sources: managers, peers, clients

## Informational Interviews

### How to Request
1. Find people in roles you're interested in
2. Send a concise, personalized message
3. Ask for 15-20 minutes of their time
4. Prepare specific questions

### Good Questions to Ask
- What does a typical day look like?
- What skills are most important for this role?
- What advice would you give someone starting out?
- What do you wish you knew earlier in your career?
