# Resume Writing Guide

## Resume Structure

### 1. Contact Information
- Full name
- Professional email
- Phone number
- LinkedIn profile
- Portfolio/GitHub (if applicable)

### 2. Professional Summary
- 2-3 sentences highlighting your value
- Include years of experience
- Mention key skills and achievements
- Tailor to target role

### 3. Skills Section
- Technical skills relevant to the job
- Software and tools proficiency
- Certifications and specializations
- Language skills if applicable

### 4. Work Experience
- Use action verbs to start bullet points
- Quantify achievements with numbers
- Focus on impact and results
- Include relevant keywords

### 5. Education
- Degree and major
- University name and graduation year
- Relevant coursework (for recent graduates)
- GPA if strong (3.5+)

## Writing Strong Bullet Points

### Formula
Action Verb + Task + Result/Impact

### Examples
- "Increased sales by 25% through implementation of new CRM system"
- "Reduced processing time by 40% by automating data entry workflows"
- "Led team of 5 developers to deliver project 2 weeks ahead of schedule"

## ATS Optimization

### Keywords
- Include keywords from job description
- Use standard section headings
- Avoid unusual formatting
- Save as PDF or DOCX

### Formatting Tips
- Clean, professional layout
- Consistent fonts and spacing
- 1-2 pages maximum
- No graphics or tables
